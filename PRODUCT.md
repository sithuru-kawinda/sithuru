# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Technical hiring managers, senior developers, and recruiters evaluating Sithuru Kawinda as a candidate or collaborator. They scan portfolios fast — forming a confident first impression in under 60 seconds. They are technically literate and can tell a thoughtfully designed site from a template immediately.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that communicates Sithuru Kawinda's technical identity and depth. Success means a visitor leaves feeling they reviewed someone serious and skilled — not just another developer with a downloaded theme.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, Bold, Refined. Precise and technical with no visual noise. Confident without being loud. High-quality details that signal seniority rather than effort.

## Anti-references

- Generic Bootstrap dark portfolio templates — cyan/purple gradients on black with particles.js everywhere. Looks identical to 10,000 other developer sites.
- Over-animated "look what CSS can do" portfolios — motion that distracts from the actual work.
- Corporate-flat "company about us" pages — no personal voice, no identity.

## Design Principles

1. Show, don't tell — every design choice reinforces technical credibility without stating it
2. Precision over decoration — tight typography, deliberate spacing, no gratuitous glow effects
3. Signal seniority through restraint — the work is the star; the design frames it without competing
4. First screen is everything — the above-the-fold experience must earn immediate respect
5. No borrowed aesthetics — if the palette or layout could belong to any other dev portfolio, rework it

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Sufficient color contrast on all text. Keyboard-navigable. No essential information conveyed by color alone.
